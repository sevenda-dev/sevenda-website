FAQ

Frequently asked questions

The extension notifies you when you reach 80% of your limit. You can purchase add-ons on demand or upgrade to the next plan. There are no automatic blocks without prior notice.

The more users on your workspace, the less you pay per seat. With 2–5 users you pay €13/user/mo (annual); with 6–20 users the price drops to €10/user/mo. Volume discounts apply automatically when you add members.

Yes. Sevenda limits apply to platform features (sessions, exports, retention). Single-track plans give full access to your chosen mode. The Suite plan unlocks both Process and Analytics in one workspace — ideal for consultants who produce BPMN deliverables and GTM audits for the same clients.

Yes. Upgrades are immediate. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. All data within new plan limits is preserved.
